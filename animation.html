Brickfilms

Over the past decade I have created a number of LEGO stop motion animations (AKA "brickfilms"). For more information about brickfilming, check out www.bricksinmotion.com, where I am a member and forum administrator under the username "Squash". To check out more of my brickfilms, see my Youtube channel.

The film below, Face, was produced entirely over the course of one day for the annual Twenty-four Hour Animation Competition. It received fourth place out of sixty-eight films.
